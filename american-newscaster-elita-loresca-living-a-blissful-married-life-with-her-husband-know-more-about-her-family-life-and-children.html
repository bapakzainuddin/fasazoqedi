Elita Loresca's Career and Net Worth

Loresca started her professional career as a news associate at KCBS-TV. Later, she joined KGET-TV in January 2001 and worked in the network until August 2002.

She reported the two most destructive hurricane seasons. In 2004, she started her job at WSVN Miami forecasting the hurricane season with Hurricane Frances the same year.

She played a key role covering Hurricane Katrina, Hurricane Rita, and Hurricane Wilma from the National Hurricane Center.
