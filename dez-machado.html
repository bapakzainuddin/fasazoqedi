Love Life of Dez Machado

As of now, Dez Machado is dating. She is in a romantic relationship with her partner in crime, Renato. Her boyfriend Renato was actually her classmate and are dating since then.

After that, Renato appeared in several vlogs, and Instagram feeds. Back in late 2019, the rumor of Dez and Renato being separated surfed on several media platforms and news portals. The hoax was soon cleared after Renato appeared in one of her vlogs shortly after.
