David James Williams Obituary, Age 59, of Winnemucca, Nevada, passed away

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, August 15, 2024
0SHARESShareTweet

David James Williams Obituary, Death – David James Williams, a beloved resident of Winnemucca, Nevada, passed away on November 12, 2023, at the age of 59. Born on September 24, 1964, in Vancouver, British Columbia, David touched the lives of many and left an indelible mark on the hearts of those who knew him. This article serves as a tribute to the life and legacy of a cherished husband, father, grandfather, brother, and friend. David’s journey was one of love, family, and passion. For 30 years, he was the devoted husband of Tracy Williams, and together they created a lifetime of cherished memories.

David was a loving father to Brittani (Vincint) Brown, Ryan (Emy) Williams, and Lindsey (and Peanut) Williams. His role as a grandfather brought him immense joy, as he adored his grandchildren, Logan and Remington Williams. In addition to his immediate family, David is survived by his brothers, Rod (Maggie) Williams, Don (Maria) Williams, Kevin (Katherine) Malone, sister Lisa (Don) Myers, nephews Andrew and Jacob Williams, Mike (Jessica) Myers, niece Shayla Myers (Branden), multiple great-nieces and nephews, as well as his father and mother-in-law, Butch and Diane Malone. His family was a source of strength and support throughout his life, and their bond remains unbroken even in his absence.

 A skilled craftsman, David enjoyed building model cars, ships, and planes. His enthusiasm for these projects reflected his meticulous attention to detail and creative spirit. Beyond that, David harbored a deep and passionate love for Star Trek, a passion that connected him with fellow enthusiasts and added a unique dimension to his personality. Friends and family are invited to join in celebrating the life of David James Williams at memorial services to be held at ChristLife Family Ministry (Winnemucca Boulevard across from Queso’s) on Saturday, November 18, 2023, at 11:00 am. The gathering will be an opportunity to reminisce, share stories, and honor a man who touched so many lives.

In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made to the Nevada Donor Network, reflecting David’s spirit of giving and generosity. As we come together to celebrate David’s life, let us remember the love, kindness, and unwavering devotion he showered upon his family and friends. In the face of sorrow, may we find solace in the beautiful memories he created and the lasting impact he had on those fortunate enough to know him. May David James Williams rest in peace, his legacy living on in the hearts of all who had the privilege of sharing in his remarkable journey.
