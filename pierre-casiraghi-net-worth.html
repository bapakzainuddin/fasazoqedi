Pierre Rainier Stefano Casiraghi Wiki Biography

Pierre Rainier Stefano Casiraghi was born on the 5th September 1987, in Princess Grace Hospital Centre, Monaco, and is the third son of Princess Charles of Monaco and Hannover and her second husband Stefano Casiraghi. He is seventh in the line of succession to the throne of Monaco.

How much is the net worth of Pierre Casiraghi? It has been estimated by authoritative sources that the outright size of his wealth is as much as $50 million, as of the data given in 2017. Engeco SA is the major source of Casiraghi’s fortune.

After their mother’s marriage to Prince Ernesto Augusto of Hanover, a half-sister was born, and the family settled in Fontainebleau to be closer to Paris and London.
