Eva Mendes Wiki Biography

Eva Mendes, known as Eva Mendez, is an American model, actor, singer, and businessperson with an estimated net worth of $15 million. She gained recognition for her roles in “2 Fast 2 Furious”, “Exit Wounds”, “Hitch”, and “Ghost Rider”. Mendes is also known for her support of PETA (People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals) and her involvement in various charitable causes.
