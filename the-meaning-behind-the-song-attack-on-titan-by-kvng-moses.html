The Meaning Behind The Song: Attack on Titan by Kvng Moses

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Attack on Titan by Kvng Moses is a captivating and emotionally charged song that delves into the depths of personal struggles and feelings of being lost. The lyrics paint a vivid picture of inner turmoil, frustration, and a desperate search for identity and purpose.

In the opening lines, Kvng Moses compares himself to boxing legend Muhammad Ali. This reference highlights his mindset going into the challenges he faces, viewing them not as mere fights, but as full-blown battles. He feels like he’s up against the world, with no holds barred.

The song delves into Kvng Moses’ introspection and grappling with the source of his rage. He ponders if it’s a result of his age, but the root cause remains elusive. He describes feeling trapped in a cage and struggling with a reactive nature, even on his off days. This passage portrays a sense of restless dissatisfaction and a longing for something more.

The following lines reveal the depth of Kvng Moses’ emotional turmoil. He questions why he is the way he is, despite having everything to prove. These feelings of self-doubt and the burning need for validation shine through, leaving him emotionally drained and lost.

Kvng Moses conveys a sense of vulnerability and a yearning for connection. He mentions the fear of uncertainty and the struggle to look in the mirror, as if avoiding his own reflection. This fear stems from the desperation to find help while appearing strong and self-reliant.

The chorus repeats the phrase “I been feeling lost” as a haunting refrain, emphasizing the central theme of the song. It showcases the singer’s inner turmoil and the overwhelming desire to find direction and purpose.

Reflecting on my own experiences, I resonate deeply with the raw and emotional journey Kvng Moses takes us on in “Attack on Titan.” There have been times in my life when I felt like I was drowning in my own thoughts, desperately searching for something or someone to anchor me.

The lyrics evoke a strong sense of isolation and a disconnect from the world, as if the singer is drifting aimlessly. This sentiment reminds me of moments when I questioned who I was and where I was headed. The yearning for guidance and the fear of never finding it resonates with anyone who has experienced a sense of being lost.

Despite the darker aspects of the song’s themes, there is an undercurrent of resilience and determination. Kvng Moses expresses a desire to rise above his circumstances and seek growth through music. This speaks to the power of creativity and self-expression as tools to navigate the complexities of life.

In conclusion, “Attack on Titan” by Kvng Moses is a personal and introspective song that explores the depths of one’s struggle with self-identity, purpose, and yearning for connection. The lyrics capture the raw emotions associated with feeling lost, while also expressing a resilient spirit. This song serves as a powerful reminder that even in our darkest moments, we can find solace and strength through self-expression and a relentless pursuit of self-discovery.
