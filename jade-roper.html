Jade Roper's Current Relationship Status Is Married; She Is A Mother Of Two Child.

Posted by Kelle Repass on Friday, September 6, 2024

Jade Roper is an American Reality TV star and a model who is best known for appearing on season 19 of The Bachelor and season 2 of Bachelor in Paradise. Not only this, but she is also the founder of the cosmetics brand Naturally Jade Cosmetics which was launched in the year 2015.

Born on 19 December 1986 in Denver, Colorado, the U.S, Jade holds American nationality and belongs to the white ethnicity. Likewise, she is the daughter of Laren Roper and Kim Roper and is the sister of a brother Zach Roper.

Jade Roper And Tanner Tolbert's Married Life 

Model, aged 23 and a reality star aged 32, is sparking their marital relationship since 2016 with no rumors of separation. This perfect couple officiated as a life partner on 24 January 2016 in front of 150 guests, including family members and friends, and many co-stars of Bachelors.

The groom and bride's special event took place in St. Regis Monarch Beach in California. The bride stunned in a luxe Galia Lahav bridal gown, featuring a lovely lace bodice, an open back, a cathedral length veil, and a sultry V-neckline while the groom wore a black suit.

First Meeting

Now, talking about her wife and husband's first met, Roper met Tanner Tolbert in the 2nd season of Bachelor in Paradise. During the season, Tanner proposed Jade bringing out the engagement ring in a romantic way at Mexica Resort in 2015.

Since the day, Jade and Tolbert have maintained a perfect relationship. Currently, the entire family living joyfully with no sign of difficulties and problems.

Blessed with Two Babies

The Bachelor star and her husband Tanner welcomed their first daughter named Emerson Avery on 21 August 2017. As of now, baby Emerson is 2 years.

 And recently on  31 July 2019, this lovely lovebird blessed with the baby boy Brooks Easton. With People magazine, the proud dad Tolbert said that their bundle of joy baby boy weighed 7 lbs, 9 ounces, and is 20 inches long during birth. 

Miscarriage With Second Baby

The Bachelor alum revealed the heartbreak news on Wednesday, 26th February 2019, via Instagram of losing their unborn child. The mother claimed that she and her husband lost the baby during their season 2 on Bachelor in Paradise, 

"I had a miscarriage. These words have burned inside me for over three years," Roper wrote. "I was honestly so scared to share our story, because I have always had this inner voice that’s said I don’t matter as much, especially when it comes to my struggles or my pain."

The reality star has now decided to share the story in order to help others who may have the same grief of losing their unborn baby. 

Career Swap, Modeling to Acting 

At a very young age, Roper moved to Los Angeles and soon started her career as a cosmetics developer. And, later she worked as a fashion model and served for Playboy for many times. In addition, TV Star has worked as an Altruistic Cosmetics business manager and as a phlebotomist in a hospital.

In 2014, the 32-year-old made her TV debut by starring on the hit reality TV show of ABC’s, The Bachelor season 19.  In the series, she shared the screen with fellow actress, Tenley Molzahn. She also appeared on season 2 of Bachelor in Paradise in 2015. She further appeared at The Bachelor at 20: A Celebration of Love as one of the contestants on The Bachelor in 2016.
