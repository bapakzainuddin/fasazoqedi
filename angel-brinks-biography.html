Who is Angel Brinks?

Angel Brinks is an American reality television personality and a famous fashion designer.

Currently, she is the co-founder and CEO of Angel Brinks company.

Angel Brinks: Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

Brinks was born in Los Angeles on October 19, 1982. She became interested in the world of fashion and glamor in her childhood years. Her father and mother’s names are also unknown.

Additionally, she began taking ballet lessons at the age of three. She is of American nationality. Furthermore, she belongs to a mixed ethnic background of Armenian and Italian.

Talking about her education, there is no detailed information relating to her educational background.

Is Angel Brinks active on social media?

Angel Brinks is active on social media. She has a huge number of followers on social networking sites such as Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter.

Angel Brinks: Career, Salary, Net Worth

Brinks initially launched her own fashion line in the year 2010. Later, her works gained recognition after her designs were featured in the New York Fashion Week in 2012 and Facet Studio’s Fashion Show.

Apart from her career in designing, Brinks is also a reality television personality. She appeared in the TV series ‘Basketball Wives LA’ in 2015.

Over the years, Angel Brinks has dressed numerous famous personalities like Blac Chyna, Amber Rose, and Nicki Minaj.

Furthermore, she announced recently that she is planning on launching an organic skincare line featuring cleansers and body scrubs.

Brinks is also a social media enthusiast and has amassed a huge number of followers on social sites including Instagram and Twitter.
