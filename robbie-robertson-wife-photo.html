Robbie Robertson was a Canadian musician who was known for his work for Bob Dylan in the mid-late 1960s and early-mid 1970s as lead guitarist. He was a member of The Band and his work was instrumental in creating the Americana music genre. His name is listed on the 59th number in Rolling Stone magazine’s list of the 100 greatest guitarists.

Robbie Robertson Wife Photo

Robbie Robertson was married to Dominique Bourgeois with whom he got married on 24 March 1968. Later they divorced but he had with her three children including two daughters named Alexandra and Delphine and a son Sebastian.

Robbie Robertson New Wife

Did Robbie Robertson have children?
